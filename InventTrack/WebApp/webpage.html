How it works

Phone sees it.
Mac processes it.
Rufus orders it.

STEP 01
📱
Phone detects motion

The phone detects pixel changes when the door opens, triggering a high-res capture sent to the FastAPI backend.

STEP 02
🧠
Mac runs Vision AI

GPT-4o Vision parses the image into structured JSON and creates a natural language prompt for Amazon's Rufus AI.

STEP 03
🛒
Instant Restock

Copy the auto-generated command and paste it directly into Rufus on Amazon for agentic brand selection and checkout.
